Well today was a productive day: I changed the rear brake pads and rotors for my car today.

{% img left /images/car_repair_8bit.jpg 325 %} Yes, I know this is not as great an automotive accomplishment as rebuilding an engine. However, I find something innately satisfying with getting your hands dirty and figuring out how to do something you normally don’t do. I have always enjoyed figuring out how to fix things and perhaps that is what drew me into computers and software. <!– more –> I believe we have a lot of technology (cars being one of them) that most of us use and take for granted. When this technology needs to be maintained/repaired many of us lack the knowledge to do so. My personal goal to counter this lack of ‘automotive know-how’ is to fix whatever can on my car, whenever I can – and hopefully save money in the process.
